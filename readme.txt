=== Download Fluent Conference Videos ===
Contributors: mintindeed, pmcdotcom
Requires at least: 3.6
Tested up to: 3.6
License: GPLv2

WP-CLI script for downloading videos of the Fluent 2013 conference and embedding them into posts.

You'll need to purchase the videos yourself, and save the info to `fluent.csv`.
